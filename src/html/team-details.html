Responsibilities

Charlie Cichetti strives to find synergy amongst SIG’s core green building services: Training, Consulting, and Engineering Services. His focus also includes Business Development and Strategy for the company as well as Client Relationships. Charlie truly is a technical expert, having worked with multiple rating systems including LEED NC, CS, CI, EB, schools, WELL, fitwel, and also millions of SF of ENERGY STAR and Cx/RCx projects.

Experience:

Charlie has more than 16 years of experience in commercial construction, real estate development, project management, and LEED consulting. He has directed multi-million dollar office, warehouse, specialty, and condo construction projects. Prior to being a LEED Consultant and Trainer, his background started as a General Contractor and transitioned to Real Estate when he worked for Opus Corporation, a $2.2B leading commercial real estate and design-build developer. Opus was an early leader within the LEED movement and he gained invaluable experience from an Owner’s perspective.

Since transitioning into the Green Building Industry, Charlie has worked on over 56 Million SF of LEED, ENERGY STAR, and Cx/RCx projects for clients ranging from SL Green, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, to the Army Corp of Engineers and various School Systems. Project types include Class A Office, Medical, Hotel, School, Stadium, and Convention Center. Mr. Cichetti was one of the first professionals to hold all of the new LEED credentials. He is also a sought after trainer and speaker on green building topics.
